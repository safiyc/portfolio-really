About Me

My name is Safiy Cham. I am a web developer located in the Pacific Northwest. My coding journey began at a coding bootcamp. Now done with the bootcamp curriculum, I continue to build projects and solve coding problems to keep my skills strong and growing. I attend meetups and conferences frequently to surround myself in the world of web development and give myself exposure to new technologies. I have coding experience in a variety of technologies.

Besides programming, I enjoy building scenes and models using 3D softwares. I make time for my creative side to keep myself balanced and calibrated. If I'm not 3D modeling, my other outlets are doodling in my sketchbook or digital sketchbook. I'm slowly adding playing the guitar in the mix.

Other activities I enjoy include trying new hiking trails, running, and video gaming... nowadays with video games, I'm moreso drafting game design ideas, rather than actually playing them.
